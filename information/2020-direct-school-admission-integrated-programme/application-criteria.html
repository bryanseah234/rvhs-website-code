Application Criteria


  1. Applicants must fulfil the following criteria to be shortlisted for school-based selection test and interview:
  • Good overall academic performance for P5 Year-End and P6 results (if available) 
  • Good grade for Chinese Language in P5 Year-End and P6 results (if available)
  • Relevant supporting information is to be keyed in via the MOE DSA Application Portal
