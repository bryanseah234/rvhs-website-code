Financial Assistance Schemes

MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS)

The Ministry of Education has enhanced the MOE FAS to increase the income ceiling to $2,500 wef Mar 2012. To be eligible for MOE FAS under the new criteria, the gross household income should not be more than $2,500 per month or the per capita income should not be more than $625 per month. Details on the eligibility and benefits for recipients of the MOE FAS can be found in the table below:

Eligibility

Benefits

  • Applicant must be Singapore citizen
  • Families must meet one of the following criteria:
    • Gross Household Income (GHI) should not be more than $2500 per month; or
    • Per capita income (PCI) should not be more than $625 per month

  • Full waiver of school fees ($5 for secondary levels and $6 for JC levels)
  • Full waiver of standard miscellaneous fees ($10 per month for secondary levels and $13.50 per month for JC levels)
  • Free textbooks and school attire for secondary levels. Bursary of $750 for JC levels
  • S120 transport credit per annum for students taking public transport
  • School Meal Programme for secondary levels

If you meet the criteria stated above and would like to receive financial assistance, please complete the attached form and submit together with the supporting documents to the General Office.
 
For further assistance or clarifications, please email to Ms Fan Suet Yeng (Administration Manager) at FAN_Suet_Yeng@schools.gov.sg.
