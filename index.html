ANNOUNCEMENT

DSA 2020 (RVHS Integrated Programme)

DSA 2020 selection (for entry to RVHS Integrated Programme 2021) will be carried out from 12 May 2020. Applications are to be made online through the MOE DSA Portal.

We will not accept submissions at the General Office.

Applications must be completed by 5 June 2020.

RVHS-IP DSA e-conferencing will be carried out from 1 July to 14 Sept 2020

Information on DSA 2020 will be available on the MOE DSA portal on 11 May 2020.  Do look out for the MOE press release and our website updates.

DSA 2020 (RVHS Junior College Programme)

DSA 2020 selection (for entry to RVHS Junior College Programme 2021) will be carried out from 12 May 2020. Applications are through our online platform here.

We will not accept submissions at the General Office.

Applications must be completed by 15 June 2020.

RVHS-JC DSA e-conferencing will be carried out from 29 June to 11 Aug 2020

Information on DSA 2020 will be available on the MOE DSA portal on 11 May 2020.  Do look out for the MOE press release and our website updates.
